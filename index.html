Kia ora!

As well as my strong fundamental tech stack, I’m an expert in building strong relationships with clients and stakeholders, providing strategic analysis of complex problems, and facilitating projects through dynamic challenges to strict deadlines.

Other than web development I love playing tabletop roleplaying games of all shapes and sizes, drinking quality beverages with my nearest and dearest, making “mixtapes” (aka playlists), or reading just about anything from comic books to fantasy novels to cultural studies.
